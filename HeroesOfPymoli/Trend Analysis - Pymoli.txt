*** Heroes Of Pymoli ***

Key Observations from generated dataframes in Pymoli Project:

1. According to the GENDER DEMOGRAPHICS & PURCHASE ANALYSIS (by gender)  dataframe, the number of male players are very high at 84% of Total players and so are the purchases.

2. From AGE DEMOGRAPHICS & PURCHASE ANALYSIS(by Age), we do see a trend where the player purchases are very high between the age group 20-24 and very less number of purchases in the age group 40+.
